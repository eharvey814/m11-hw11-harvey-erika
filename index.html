About Me

I was born and raised in Central Florida and graduated from UCF in 2006 with a Bachelor’s degree in Physical Education. My career path has included teaching dance in the public school system, being a stay‑at‑home mom, running a small online business, and managing my family’s Airbnb. Currently, I work as a dance fitness instructor at a local barre studio and teach dance in after‑school programs in Orlando. Most recently, I have returned to school as a full‑time student at the University of Florida, where I am pursuing a Master’s degree in Web Design. This program supports my transition from teaching into digital media and design, allowing me to combine creativity, problem‑solving, and user‑focused design in a new career direction.

Current Focus

My current focus is on building knowledge in web design as I transition from teaching into the digital landscape. I aim to create a portfolio that reflects my growth and showcases my developing skills.
